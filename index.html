A Revolutionary New Theory

Welcome to the Time Cube site. Time Cube is the brainchild of Gene Ray, engineer and philosopher, formulated in 1997. The core hypothesis of Time Cube is that linear time is a misconception: Earth actually passes through four simultaneous days, instead of merely one. This theory is yet to reach the status of mainstream scientific knowledge.

History

In 1884, the International Meridian Conference met in Washington, D.C. to discuss the location of the prime meridian, i.e. the international standard for zero degrees longitude. There could, of course, be only one of these, as the delegates present were unaware of the fact that there are actually four simultaneous days. This is an error.
